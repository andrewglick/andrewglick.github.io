The Holdup is a long-form improv group performing in rooms with chairs around Los Angeles. Its members first met at USC, where they performed with Second Nature, The Merry Men, and Commedus Interruptus. Before that, they were all just kids with dreams.

They enjoyed a twelve week winning streak at the Upright Citizens Brigade Cagematch and have performed at the "Del Close Marathon" in New York and "Out of Bounds" in Austin.
